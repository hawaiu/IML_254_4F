MONSTA X

Fandom: Monbebe


MONSTA X is a South Korean boy group consisting of six members: Shownu, Minhyuk, Kihyun, Hyungwon, Joohoney, and I.M. The group was formed through the survival program NO.MERCY and debuted on May 14, 2015, under Starship Entertainment. As of February 26, 2019, they are also signed under the US label Maverick Agency.

Initially, the group had seven members, but Wonho departed on October 31, 2019, following some controversies. Despite this, MONSTA X has continued to thrive, captivating fans worldwide with their dynamic performances and versatile music.

Why I love Monsta X

As someone who loves energetic and powerful music, MONSTA X caught my attention immediately. Their songs are a perfect blend of intense beats and catchy melodies, making it impossible not to dance along. Each member brings something unique to the group, creating a perfect synergy.

Members

Shownu


Position: Leader, Main Dancer, Lead Vocalist

Shownu is known for his incredible dance skills and calm demeanor. His stage presence is magnetic, and he always gives his best in every performance.

Minhyuk


Position: Sub Vocalist, Visual

Minhyuk is the mood maker of the group, always bringing positivity and energy. His bright personality shines through in variety shows, making him a fan favorite.

Kihyun


Position: Main Vocalist

As the main vocalist, Kihyun’s voice is both powerful and emotional. His ability to convey deep emotions through his singing is one of the reasons why their ballads are so touching.

Hyungwon


Position: Lead Dancer, Sub Vocalist, Sub Rapper, Visual, Center

Hyungwon is known for his striking visuals and charismatic stage presence, Hyungwon is also a talented DJ, adding another layer to his artistry. By the way, he is my bias!

Jooheon


Position: Main Rapper, Sub Vocalist

The main rapper and producer, Joohoney, is known for his versatility. His rap lines are impactful, and his stage performances are always electrifying. He's been produce most of their songs since debut.

I.M


Position: Lead Rapper, Sub Vocalist, Maknae

The youngest member, I.M, has a deep, distinctive voice that adds a unique flavor to their music. His lyrics are thoughtful and introspective, often reflecting his personal experiences.
