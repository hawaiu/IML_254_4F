Book Bliss: My Reading Journey

Welcome to my little corner dedicated to the wonderful world of books. I’m a huge bookworm, and I’ve loved reading for as long as I can remember. It all started with bedtime stories my mom and dad read to me when I was little. Since then, books have been my best buddies.

I'm into all sorts of books, from magical fantasies to real-life stories. But what really gets me going are romance and slice-of-life tales. Authors like Anjell, Teme Abdullah, Rehan Makhtar, and Norhafsah Hamid are my absolute favorites. Their stories make me feel all warm and fuzzy inside!

But I'm not picky reader. I'll read anything that catches my eye. Whether it's a thrilling mystery, a journey through history, or a peek into modern life, I'm up for it! Each book I read opens up a whole new world, and I love getting lost in them.

Books aren't just stories to me; they're like magic portals to different places and times. They teach me new things and help me understand the world better. Whenever I crack open a book, I know I'm in for an adventure, and that's the best feeling in the world!
